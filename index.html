about me
I'm a developer, designer, and adventurer. I've spent several years as a speech therapist. During that time, I've continued designing on the side and picked up web design and development. I love making beautiful websites that work for users!
Outside of development and design, I love travel, food, and taking a lot of pictures of my cat. My pursuit of trying new foods and finding more color in my life has taken me around the world. Even so, I always find myself back in Charlotte, the place I call home, and my mom’s cooking is still my favorite! Take a look around!
